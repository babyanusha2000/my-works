HTML or HyperText Markup Language,the backbone of the web,is a language of structure
	and semantics.Its tags define the content and layout of web pages,facilitating
	seamless interaction between users and information.From simple paragraphs to complex
	forms and multimedia,HTML empowers creators to build engaging digital experiences that
	transcend boundaries and connect people worldwide.

				      		"HTML is the skeleton of your webpages"
